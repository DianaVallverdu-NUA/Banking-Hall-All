UK Solar Panels

You are seeing the work by Creative Computing Level 5 Students. This project displays the amount of solar panels installed in the UK from 2010 to 2024. The participants in this project were Robert Besala, Curtis Hall, Daisy Hind, Amy Liddington, Tori O'Connor and Laurie Millings.

Play with the midi controller to change the visual quality. Pressing the Next / Previous Track buttons will change the work displayed.
